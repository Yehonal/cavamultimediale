The Princes Guaimario III and Guaimario IV of Salerno donate in perpetuity, to 
Abbot Alferio, the Cave of Arsiccia and all the land around it, bordered by the 
River Selano, and the rivulets of Gegnulo and Sasso Vivo, as well as freedom from 
all taxes for its inhabitants. Thus started the feudal power of Badia.
