Born in Cava in 1871, he died on 11 January 1967. 
Most of his works were of a religious nature, 
painting St Francis of Paola in the Basilica del-
l'Olmo, as well as pictures at the Church of St. 
Mary of Rovo and at the chapel of Castagneto. He 
performed restoration work at the Duomo in Cava 
and at the Church of St Rita alla Speranza in 
Naples, as well as at another fifteen churches in 
various locations in Cava and Naples.
