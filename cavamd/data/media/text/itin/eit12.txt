To the northwest of the valley of Cava, some three 
kilometres from the city itself, on the boundaries of 
Nocera Superiore, and situated at the foot of Monte 
Finestra and Monte San Angelo, is the hill of San 
Martino. Rising to a height of 577 metres, it was 
first mentioned in a document originating from 
Prince Gisulfo II, in 1058.
