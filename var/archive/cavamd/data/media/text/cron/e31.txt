The Opening Ceremony for the Ausino aqueduct wass held in August of this year. 
In his speech to the citizens of Cava, Deputy Errico De Marinis told the crowd 
“Along with my fellow citizens, I salute this gushing water, this essential element 
of life, for which the City has long waited. I hope, as did the Marquis Atenolfi, that 
all works dreamed of come true.”
